Angela Wu

Thanks for visiting my webpage!

My mathematical interests are mainly symplectic and contact geometry and topology. I am particularly interested in Legendrian knots, Weinstein manifolds, and Lagrangian cobordisms.

I am a tenure track assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at Bucknell University.

Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Louisiana State University. My postdoc mentor was Shea Vela-Vick.

I obtained my PhD from University College London under the guidance of Steven Sivek at Imperial College London through the London School of Geometry and Number Theory.
